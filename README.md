## About

Year-round accounting services division of PCS Tax Service & Academy, a 22-year tax preparation business based in Dallas-Fort Worth. This site promotes bookkeeping, payroll, financial reporting, and CFO-level advisory services to small and mid-size business owners nationwide.

## Services Promoted

Monthly bookkeeping, payroll processing, quarterly tax filings, annual tax preparation, financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow), budget analysis, and CFO-level strategic recommendations.
